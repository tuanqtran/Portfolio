About Me

My little story.

  • 2009-2011

    My Humble Beginnings

    Originally after I graduated from High School, I decided to attend Lamar State College - Port Arthur with absolutely no conception towards my future goals. For about two years, I have taken classes ranging from fines art to physical anatomy.

  • 2011-2015

    A new leaf

    I later attended Lamar University in Beaumont, Texas in pursuit of an Bachelors of Science in Electrical Engineer. Throughout my college years in Beaumont, I have met many extraordinary people with amazing backgrounds.

  • January 2016

    The Big Move

    My wife and I decided to move to a larger city in order to pursue higher opportunities. We eventually found a place we now call home and later gain an additional family member to our group, Milo our little Sugar Glider!

  • July 2016

    UT Coding Bootcamp

    During my stay within Austin, I have gotten to learn how to program with PLCs (RxLogix 500 with Allen Bradley) and also got into web development. I am now a proud coding student at UT Austin where I will gain the strong fundamental foundations to become an highly proficient Full-Stack Web Developer.
